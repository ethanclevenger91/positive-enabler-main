Mindfulness Meditation

This isn't like meditation after a yoga class. 

Mindfulness meditation is a deeper therapeutic technique for reducing stress and calming the mind. Guided Meditation Sessions are for anyone who wants to enjoy some peace & calm, and crawl into a stress-free bubble in the middle of a crazy world & work-week. Meditation is a key component of the newly emerging holistic health care system.

A chiropractor helps heal inflammation in the body. 
A meditation practitioner can help relieve inflammation held in the mind, the most common symptoms of which are stress, depression & anxiety.

Group sessions include:

• Brief introduction to the therapy of mindfulness 
(10-15 minutes) 

• Guided meditation, including peaceful imagery & stress reduction techniques (20 minutes) 

• Discussion/QA after (15 minutes) 

Individual Sessions are also available
