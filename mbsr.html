MBSR

Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction
Do you want to cultivate a deeper sense of happiness, experience less stress in your life and improve your relationships?

When we're living in the past, we often experience feelings of depression; when we're thinking about the future, we feel anxious.

Mindfulness is quickly catching on a very powerful tool for reducing stress, calming the mind, and living in the moment.

What is MSBR?
MBSR is a technique designed by Jon Kabat-Zinn and taught at the Center for Mindfulness - University of Massachusetts Medical School. There is an emphasis on the mind-body connection as well as becoming aware of your own thoughts.

Through a series of specific, guided meditation techniques, we learn to still the "monkey mind" and become more fully present in the moment.

The classes ad also emphasis interaction and participation from the whole group. So more than meditation.

One-on-one mindfulness sessions
In one-on-one sessions, the ephasisis can either be on Stress Reduction, or Cognitive Therapy techniques.
